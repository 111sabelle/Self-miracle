Time is linear, never interrupted. If every second of time were to be simultaneously presented, unfolding into a blueprint that generates freely, what kind of canvas would you choose? A new story would also be freely told.

"Miracle Search" is a fantasy blueprint based on three real exploration stories. The nine-hour length breaks boundaries, just as the characters in the story face unknown challenges. Expanding on Cheng Ran's nine-hour experimental film images, it imagines time (30,000 seconds) as a tiled pattern restructured and regenerated by algorithms. When time is restructured and regenerated, it recombines into abstract patterns and color fields. Countless possibilities of time are laid out in a two-dimensional space, rearranged and regenerated.
